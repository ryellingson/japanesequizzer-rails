## Number Guess

---

Follow the on screen instructions and you'll be sure to win this one.

Make sure to **WRITE OUT** the number in the language.

いち, さんじゅう, ろくじゅうなな = ✅ 😄  
1, 30, 67 = ❌ 😣

Remember in **Japanese** you can use Kanji, Kana, or Romaji!

For example: 5

Kanji = 五 <br>
Kana = ご <br>
Romaji = go

Hit **Play** to get started!

Good Luck!!!
